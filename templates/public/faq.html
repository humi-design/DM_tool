FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Viraly.

General

What is Viraly?

Viraly is an AI-powered social media automation platform that handles your DMs, comments, and messages automatically. Our AI responds to inquiries, captures leads, and converts conversations - all while sounding human and working 24/7.

How is Viraly different from other automation tools?

Unlike traditional automation tools that require complex triggers and rules, Viraly uses AI that automatically understands context, intent, and sentiment. You don't need to set up 50+ rules - just connect and let AI work. It's simpler, faster, and more effective.

What platforms does Viraly support?

Currently, Viraly supports Instagram (DMs, Comments, Stories) and Facebook Messenger. WhatsApp support is coming soon. We're also working on TikTok integration.

Setup & Getting Started

How long does it take to set up?

Most users are up and running in under 5 minutes. Simply connect your Instagram account, choose your goals (capture leads, answer questions, make sales), and watch the AI work. No coding, no automation rules, no technical skills required.

Do I need to set up triggers or automation rules?

No! That's the beauty of Viraly. Unlike other tools, you don't need to set up complex triggers or automation rules. Our AI automatically understands what users want and responds appropriately. Just connect and let it work.

Is my account safe? Will this get me banned?

Absolutely safe. Viraly is a Meta Verified Partner and our automation operates within each platform's terms of service. We use official APIs and have built-in safeguards to ensure your account stays secure. Thousands of users have been using Viraly for years without issues.

AI & Features

How does the AI work?

Our AI is trained on millions of social media conversations and understands context, intent, and sentiment. It can recognize returning users, spot buying signals in comments, handle objections, and respond naturally. It's like having a highly trained customer service rep that never sleeps.

Can I customize how the AI responds?

Yes! You can train the AI with your brand voice, preferred responses, and specific offers. You can also set goals (capture emails, book appointments, make sales) and the AI will optimize its responses accordingly.

What is "Intent Detection"?

Intent Detection is our AI's ability to understand what users want. For example, when someone comments "How much?", the AI recognizes this as a buying signal and responds with pricing information. When a returning user messages, it remembers their previous conversation and greets them personally.

Does Viraly work during Instagram Lives?

Yes! During live streams, Viraly monitors comments and can auto-reply or suggest responses for you to approve. This is perfect for handling the flood of questions that come in during a live session.

Pricing & Billing

How much does Viraly cost?

Plans start at $29/month for the Starter plan (1 Instagram account, 1,000 messages). Pro plan at $79/month offers 3 accounts and 10,000 messages. Agency plan at $199/month provides unlimited everything including white-label.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You get full access to all features during the trial period.

What happens if I exceed my message limit?

You'll receive notifications when you reach 80% of your limit. You can upgrade to a higher plan, or messages will pause until your next billing cycle. We never charge overages without explicit consent.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Cancel with one click from your dashboard. No questions asked, no cancellation fees. If you cancel, you'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

Security & Privacy

Is my data secure?

Yes. We're SOC2 certified and use 256-bit encryption for all data. Your conversations and business information are never stored in plain text. We never sell your data to third parties.

Who owns my data?

You do. All your data belongs to you. You can export or delete your data at any time. We only use anonymized, aggregated data for improving our AI models.

Is Viraly GDPR compliant?

Yes, we're fully GDPR compliant. We offer data residency options, right to deletion, and transparent data processing practices.
